ACCESS DENIED

This project explores the struggles and hardships experienced by underprivileged children in India to gain an equal and meaningful education.

YOU are invited to uncover the nuances and hidden complexities by getting a chance to be in their shoes.

What will you, as one of the children, choose to prioritize today?
The choices are tough and the stakes high.

Join us as we embark on this compelling journey, where every decision is a step forward in the path of survival.



DISCLAIMER



The stories presented in this work are inspired by true events and experiences. However, to protect the privacy and anonymity of the individuals involved, names, characters, and specific plot details have been altered. These stories have been artistically curated to illuminate various aspects of the challenges faced by underprivileged students in the realm of education. While the narratives draw from real-life situations, they do not represent an exhaustive or universally applicable account. Each student's journey is unique, and the hardships depicted here may or may not mirror the experiences of all individuals. The stories featured are a result of ethnographic research and interviews, capturing the essence of real struggles encountered by specific households while shedding light on broader issues within the context of education.
